Blast to the Past

Wow! It has been a while since we’ve had a new post!

But this new post will be telling you about our new website. It’s not very hard to tell that at The Phoenix Project we appreciate retro things, so we have carried that value over to our web design.

Our old website was built on WordPress, which in many ways is a very capable tool, but for our purposes it is very bloated, slow, and somewhat difficult to work with. This new site has been handcrafted in HTML, CSS, and a small amount of JavaScript.

We hope to start bringing over more content to this site and writing new posts soon! But that’s all for now. Please tell us what you think!
